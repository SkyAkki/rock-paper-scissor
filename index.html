Janken じゃんけん
  1. Click on Let's Play
  2. Shaisho wa guu is your cue to get ready.
  3. Make your choice on Jan Ken
  4. Make sure to select before Pon
  1. Rock in Japanese is 岩 (Iwa). In Janken we say Guu. ✊🏽
  2. Paper in Japanese is 紙 (Kami). In Janken we say Paa. ✋🏽
  3. Scissors in Japanese is 鋏 (Hasami). In Janken we say Choki. ✌🏽
  4. 最初はぐう (Saisho Wa Guu ) means "starting with rock", and cues both the player to show rock.
  5. あいこでしょ! (Aiko desho) means 'It's a draw, isn't it?", and on しょ (sho) both players play again.
